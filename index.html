Greetings and welcome.

This website came into existence because of my slight interest in fishing. It's a pretty cool sport and hobby. Unfortunately, I have no experience when it comes to fishing so I planned a trip out to Indian Springs State Park to practice.

I invite you and anyone else interested in this camping adventure up north. It'll span for a week in the month of April of next year.

Even if there's just a small group of people going out with me during that time to have our fishing trip it'll still be a grand experience. I can't wait to catch and release some or maybe cook some up. It might even feel like a true camping adventure then.

We'll try to catch any native fish in the area if possible and the fishing won't stop when the sun goes down. I heard some fish only swim at night so that'll be more fun to be hand but we'll take breaks we do have a week to figure out the basics and actually get to fishing so no rush!
